About Us

Our ambition is to lead the hospitality industry into a new era of evolution

We live in a period where the most important asset for a company is the data they produce. The goal for hospitality solutions ai is to help you utilise your data, implementing artificial intelligence algorithms, to drive decision making.

Innovation in Hospitality

Red Roof Inn is a well known example of how predictive analytics and AI can help the hospitality industry. Combining data from airport delays and weather data they predicted which areas will have accomodation needs in the future. By opening new branches in those areas they managed to increase revenue by 10%. By using internal and external data we reinforce your decision making in order to increase revenue. Your data has the answer.
